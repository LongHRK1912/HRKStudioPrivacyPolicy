What is VitaBeat?

VitaBeat is a comprehensive health management app for Android designed to help you stay on top of your cardiovascular health and medication routines. By merging advanced camera-based heart rate monitoring with a robust medication tracking system, VitaBeat empowers users to manage their well-being without the need for expensive wearables. Built with modern Material Design 3, it offers a professional yet soothing experience for everyday health tracking.

Core Features

  • Medication Tracking: Manage complex schedules including daily doses, specific weekdays, and "as needed" (PRN) medications.
  • Smart Reminders: Never miss a dose with high-precision notifications and a 24/7 intelligent alert system.
  • Heart Rate Monitor: Measure heart rate (HR) and Heart Rate Variability (HRV) instantly using just your smartphone camera.
  • Health Insights: View detailed Heart Rate Zones (Zone 1-5) and receive actionable insights on stress levels and physiological age.
  • Stock Management: Keep track of your pill inventory with automatic refill reminders and overdose warnings.
  • Daily Health Journal: A unified timeline showing your medication adherence and vital sign check-ins.
  • Gamification: Build healthy habits with daily streaks and achievement milestones.
  • Data Export: Generate professional medical reports in PDF format to share with your healthcare provider.

Technological Innovation

VitaBeat utilizes Photoplethysmography (PPG) technology via the smartphone camera to detect tiny changes in light absorption under the skin, allowing for precise heart rate extraction. The app leverages Android's most reliable background scheduling APIs (AlarmManager & WorkManager) to ensure critical health reminders are delivered exactly on time, even when the device is in sleep mode.
