What is PicPulse?

PicPulse is a daily photo journal app that helps you capture life's moments, one day at a time. Take exactly one photo each day with automatic timestamp and GPS location tracking to create a beautiful visual diary of your life. Built entirely on Firebase's free platform with a modern, clean interface using Jetpack Compose.

Core Features

  • One photo per day - Simple, focused daily photography
  • Automatic timestamp and GPS location tracking
  • AI-powered caption suggestions (on-device)
  • Daily streak tracking and achievement badges
  • Photo history and calendar view
  • Share photos to social media
  • Light/Dark mode with Material Design
  • Daily reminders via push notifications
  • Cloud sync across devices with Firebase

Gamification & Motivation

Stay motivated with achievement badges and level progression. Build daily streaks by capturing photos consistently - earn badges for 7-day, 30-day, and 100-day streaks. Track your progress and make photography a daily habit.
