Privacy Policy

TuneDetect is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we handle data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

The application does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as name, email, account details, or location.

When you use the song recognition feature, the app uses the microphone to record a short audio sample. This audio is sent directly to the ACRCloud server for analysis and song identification.

2. How We Use the Information

The recorded audio is used solely for the purpose of identifying songs. The application does not store, modify, or use this data for any other purpose.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

The application does not share data with any third parties other than ACRCloud, which is the service provider used for processing and identifying songs.

4. User Rights

You may deny microphone access at any time. In that case, the song recognition feature will not function, but you can still use other parts of the application.

5. Data Security

TuneDetect does not store audio data on our servers or devices. All audio is sent directly to ACRCloud servers for processing.
