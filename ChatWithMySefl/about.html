Chat With Myself

Reflect on yourself — one message at a time

Our Mission

Chat With Myself helps you build a habit of emotional reflection by writing messages to your future self. When the time comes, the app returns your message like a time capsule.

Core Features

  • Send messages to your future self (1, 3, 7, 30 days).
  • Receive reminders via notifications.
  • PIN/Biometric lock for privacy.
  • Timeline for emotional tracking.
  • Optional data export/backup.
